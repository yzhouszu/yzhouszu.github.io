I am an assistant professor at Research Institute for Future Media Computing, College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University. Before Joining Shenzhen University, from Feb 2017 to June 2017, I was a postdoctoral research fellow of Video Technology Research Centre directed by Prof. Sam Kwong in Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong. I completed my Ph.D. in Deparment of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Sam Kwong. Prior to that, I completed my master's degree in Electrical Engineering at Xidian University, supervised by Prof. Licheng Jiao and Prof. Shuyuan Yang in the School of Electrical Engineering, where I was a postgraduate student in Key Laboratory of Intelligent Perception and Image Understanding of Minstry of Education . I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering at Xidian University.
